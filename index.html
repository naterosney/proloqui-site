OUR TECHNOLOGY

A multi-agent AI pipeline built for quality at scale.

At the core of Proloqui is a proprietary architecture where four specialised AI agents collaborate on every piece of outreach. Each agent has a distinct role, and together they produce messages that are personalised, relevant, and compliant.

This is not a single prompt generating bulk emails. Each agent reviews and builds on the work of the one before it, creating a quality loop that improves with every message sent.

1
Message Strategy Agent
Analyses the prospect's context, identifies the best angle, and structures a messaging strategy tailored to their role and company.
2
Personalisation Agent
Enriches the message with prospect-specific details: recent company news, hiring signals, technology stack, and decision-making context.
3
Quality Review Agent
Evaluates tone, clarity, and persuasiveness. Rewrites weak sections and ensures the message meets your brand standards.
4
Compliance Agent
Checks for regulatory compliance, spam triggers, and deliverability risks. Ensures every message is safe to send.
