ABOUT PROLOQUI AI

Sales should scale through
intelligence, not burnout.

We started Proloqui because we saw the same problem everywhere: talented sales teams buried under manual work, sending messages that never land, and never doing the one thing they are actually good at, building relationships and deeply understanding their customers. We are building the AI workforce that changes that.

OUR STORY

Built by people who understand both sides of the problem.

Modern sales teams are overloaded with tools, data, and manual tasks. Up to 70% of a seller's time is spent on non-selling work: researching prospects, crafting messages, following up across channels, and tracking responses.

The industry responded by adding more software and more people. We are removing the work entirely.

Proloqui AI was founded in January 2026 with a clear thesis: autonomous AI agents can handle the repetitive execution of outbound sales, while humans stay in control of strategy, relationships, and closing. Not another tool that adds to the stack. A system that replaces the manual work altogether.

We are building a platform where revenue generating teams upload their contacts, define their ideal customer profile, and let coordinated AI agents handle the rest: researching each prospect, scoring them by fit and timing, writing personalised value-based messages, and delivering them through the right channel at the right moment.

Every message Proloqui sends passes through a proprietary multi-agent pipeline that ensures quality, relevance, and compliance. Humans review and approve before anything goes out. The result is outbound that feels personal, lands with decision-makers, and scales without adding headcount.

WHAT DRIVES US

Our vision, mission, and approach to building the future of sales.

Our Vision

A world where revenue growth is driven by autonomous systems, not endless headcount.

Sales doesn't fail because sellers are bad at selling. It fails because humans are forced to behave like machines. We believe AI should own the execution so people can focus on thinking, relating, and closing.

Our Mission

End manual prospecting by giving every seller an autonomous AI workforce.

Proloqui replaces repetitive outbound work with coordinated AI agents that research, qualify, write, and deliver. Human control and built-in safety are present at every step.

Our Approach

AI with human control, so outbound stays personal, trusted, and effective.

We don't help teams send more messages. We remove the repetitive work entirely. Humans stay in control of strategy and closing. AI takes ownership of execution.

OUR PRINCIPLES

How we build and what we believe.

Human control, always

AI handles execution. Humans approve every message, set the strategy, and own the customer relationship. Automation without oversight is a risk we will not take.

Quality over volume

We measure success by message quality and reply rates, not by how many emails get blasted. One well-crafted message beats a hundred generic ones.

Privacy and compliance first

Every message is checked for regulatory compliance and deliverability before it goes out. We build trust by doing the right thing by default, not as an afterthought.

Built for real teams

We design for the sales managers and reps who use our product daily. Every feature solves a real workflow problem, not a hypothetical one.

Constantly improving

Our multi-agent pipeline learns from every campaign. What works gets reinforced. What doesn't gets refined. The platform gets smarter with use.

Equal partnership

Proloqui is built on a 50/50 founding partnership between commercial strategy and technical execution. Every decision reflects both perspectives.
